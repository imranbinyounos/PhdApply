# GradKit Pro - Feature Documentation

## Comprehensive Feature Overview

### 🎯 Core Application Management

#### Professor Tracking
- **Contact Information**: Store professor names, email addresses, university affiliations
- **Research Interests**: Detailed fields for research areas and specializations
- **Application Status**: Track communication status from initial research to final decision
- **Priority Levels**: Assign and visualize priority with custom color coding
- **Custom Notes**: Unlimited text fields for personal observations and fit assessment

#### University Management
- **Institution Database**: Comprehensive university and department information
- **Program Details**: Track specific graduate programs and their requirements
- **Deadline Management**: Visual deadline tracking with sorting and filtering
- **Department Contacts**: Multiple contact points within each institution

#### Scholarship & Funding
- **Funding Opportunities**: Track scholarships, fellowships, and assistantships
- **Application Requirements**: Document specific requirements for each opportunity
- **Deadline Coordination**: Integrate funding deadlines with application timelines
- **Award Tracking**: Monitor application outcomes and award notifications

### 📊 Advanced Status Management

#### Application Pipeline Stages
1. **Researching**: Initial investigation phase
   - Professor research and fit assessment
   - Program requirement gathering
   - University and department evaluation

2. **Drafting Email**: Communication preparation
   - Email template management
   - Research proposal drafting
   - Contact information verification

3. **Contacted**: Active communication phase
   - Initial contact sent
   - Follow-up scheduling
   - Response tracking

4. **Awaiting Response**: Pending phase
   - Response timeframe monitoring
   - Follow-up reminders
   - Status update notifications

5. **Interview Scheduled**: Active engagement
   - Interview preparation tracking
   - Meeting coordination
   - Follow-up planning

6. **Submitted**: Formal application phase
   - Application completion tracking
   - Document submission monitoring
   - Confirmation receipt logging

7. **Accepted/Rejected**: Decision phase
   - Outcome tracking
   - Decision analysis
   - Next step planning

#### Visual Progress Indicators
- **Color-Coded Status**: Immediate visual feedback on application progress
- **Priority Ranking**: Numerical and visual priority assignment
- **Deadline Urgency**: Time-sensitive visual indicators
- **Completion Percentage**: Overall application progress tracking
